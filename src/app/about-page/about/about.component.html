How we're and what's our mission and objectives?

ABCDE is proud to be one of the leading humanitarian and educational organizations, harnessing the talents of healthcare professionals across the United States and beyond.

  • ABCDE was founded in 1998 as an educational medical society.
  • Strive to promote professional relationships between ABCDE members and medical organizations in America, Egypt, and other countries.
  • Aspire to provide a platform for the exchange of ideas and enhancing the medical knowledge of ABCDE members and to support continuing medical education.
